## Summary

This video by Bastien from "Les Makers" compares two methods for finding clients as a freelancer: **prospection** (outbound outreach) vs **création de contenu** (content marketing).

**Key takeaways:**

**Prospection advantages:**
- Precise targeting of prospects
- Can target premium clients with budgets
- Personalized messaging
- Nearly free to execute

**Prospection disadvantages:**
- Get lost in message noise
- High competition
- Often price-based competition
- Energy-draining and time-consuming
- Requires organization for follow-ups

**Content creation advantages:**
- Builds personal brand
- Long-term visibility (content works for months/years)
- Hotter leads (they already know you)
- Less need to explain/convince
- Social media as search engine discovery

**Content creation disadvantages:**
- No control over lead quality
- Unpredictable/inconsistent lead flow

**Bastien's recommendation:** A hybrid approach - 80% content creation, 20% quality prospecting (not mass outreach). But if forced to choose, he favors content creation for developing skills and attracting clients naturally in 2026.
